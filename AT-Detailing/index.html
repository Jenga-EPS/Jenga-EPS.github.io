AT Detailing

AT Detailing is a car detailing company based out of Issaquah Washington. We pride ourselves on a hardworking team, dedicated to providing top-notch services that go beyond the conventional standards of car care. From exterior polishing to interior cleaning, AT Detailing ensures every vehicle receives meticulous attention to detail.

What sets AT Detailing apart is not just our commitment to quality, but also our genuine warmth and friendliness towards our clients. Our team understands that customer relationships are at the heart of a successful business, and we strive to create a welcoming atmosphere for everyone who interacts with us. This personalized approach fosters trust and loyalty, making customers feel like they are part of the AT Detailing family.

Moreover, AT Detailing stands out in the market by offering highly competitive prices without compromising on the quality of our services. This commitment to affordability makes our detailing packages accessible to a wide range of customers, reinforcing the fact that we are a company that prioritizes the needs of the customer. AT Detailing's dedication to excellence, combined with our friendly demeanor and competitive pricing, positions us as a standout choice for anyone seeking professional and affordable car detailing services.

Who We Are

Logan Sanborn and Alexander Tsilas, high school juniors with an insatiable passion for cars, found themselves drawn to the world of automotive aesthetics during a transformative summer break. It all began when the two friends stumbled upon a local car detailing workshop that piqued their interest. Intrigued by the meticulous process of restoring and enhancing a vehicle's appearance, Logan and Alexander decided to delve into the art of car detailing. They spent their summer attending workshops, researching techniques, and practicing their skills on their own vehicles. What started as a casual exploration soon turned into a full-fledged passion project, with the duo perfecting the art of washing, waxing, and polishing cars to a pristine finish. Their shared enthusiasm not only strengthened their friendship but also sparked a newfound entrepreneurial spirit as they began offering their detailing services to friends and neighbors. Logan and Alexander's journey into car detailing not only allowed them to refine their skills but also provided a fulfilling way to combine their love for cars with a hands-on, creative endeavor.
